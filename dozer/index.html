Stay in touch, literally

Never again worry about missing any time-critical messages

Reliability is the key metric of VPN user experience. Built upon high cost top tier international network infrastructures, Dozer delivers a well-provisioned, low latency, highly reliable global VPN network that is unparalleled in this industry. Most of your traffic reaches its destination with a single hop to the end user’s ISP, so you are always enjoying minimum congestion and maximum performance, which is essential for time-critical tasks like instant messaging etc.

As for blockage penetration, Dozer uses Shadowsocks for much greater penetration rate than any other traditional VPN protocols, like IPSec, L2TP/ IPSec, PPTP etc., which are largely blocked by countries like China. The cool thing is, even if a server is blocked, Dozer intelligent infrastructure will immediately heal itself up and reduce the connection gap down to just a few seconds.

Smoothness means speed

Eliminating ping and loss matters more than a nominal peak speed

Since most of the traffic reaches its destination with a single hop, Dozer Lines' latency and congestion are reduced to an extremely low level. Nonetheless, all Dozer Apps are equipped with Auto Split Tunneling (mainland China only for now): splitting local traffic from the outbound, letting the local goes directly while the outbound through proxy. This maximizes your experience by speeding up local traffic while ensuring penetration of the outbound.

It's latency and loss rate, not the peak speed that many are selling, that determines most of your everyday experience. All Dozer Apps are shipped with Speed Test function for you to easily find out those key metrics of both Dozer Lines and Custom Lines. Dozer automatically chooses the best line according to your location, and in most cases no hassle to switch lines later.

Ultimate security standard

We hate surveillance, censorship or blockage more than you do

Besides hiding your IP address and mixing your traffic with that of other users, Dozer also encrypts all your traffic in and out with the best in industry AES 256 Encryption, so that it can’t be read by third parties in between, such as your internet Service Provider or your local Wi-Fi operator. AES 256 is the same encryption standard adopted by the U.S. government and used by security experts worldwide to protect classified information.

We never keep sensitive information and both activity logs and connection logs eliminated. Our systems are built around NOT knowing how customers utilize our services. We cannot answer questions like “Which users connected to a given IP at a specific time?” or “Which users accessed a particular website?” Because we simply never know the answers.
